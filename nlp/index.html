Gaining Insight into Kanji through NLP

List of things to do for my research:

  • Split up the pronunciations of words and align parts of the pronunciations to the Kanji/Kana
  • Determine important features and their relationships
    • Pronunciation of syllable before and after Kanji of interest
    • Kanji/Kana type of grapheme before and after Kanji of interest
    • Meaning of vocabulary
  • Train the Bayesian Network

I am currently working on an algorithm in Python to use MeCab to parse vocabulary from one dataset and then match the pronunciations to the individual graphemes. Current problems are that some pronunciations are "irregular" and only appear for a certain vocabulary word, rendering my algorithm unable to match any pronunciations to any individual character.
