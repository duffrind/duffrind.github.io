Welcome!

I am interested in Natural Language Processing as a means to aid in the learning of a foreign language and also to reduce barriers that foreign language presents, allowing a natural flow of communication in situations where ideas are either lost in translation or otherwise completely missed. I hope to use Augmented Reality as a medium to provide the user with resources such as real-time translations and seamlessly decipher meanings into a comprehendable format.

My current coursework is:

Natural Language Processing:

I regularly meet one-on-one with a professor to talk about the techniques I learn to process language and how that relates to Data Science.

Algorithms for Data Science:

We reviewed the standard algorithms and data structures and then talked about how we can optimize them to save time and processing power. We are currently working on neural networks and machine learning algorithms.

Data Storage and Retrieval:

We dive into PostgreSQL to learn about relational databases and MongoDB for NoSQL.

Data Munging and Exploratory Analysis:

This class consists mostly of group projects in which we find datasets online and clean up the data, find the quality, summarize, and then visualize the data and come up with our own conclusions.


My goals for the future would be to either do research at graduate school or work related to Natural Language Processing and a bonus would be to take advantage of Augmented/Virtual Reality technologies.
